Myth Creatures

Urmahlullu

An urmahlullu is a fictitious and mythological lion-centaur hybrid creature. They are quadrupedal felines from the waist down and humanoids from the waist up, and have appeared in the folklore and myths of several cultures of antiquity, as well as in European art of the Middle Ages and early Renaissance.

Lamassu

The Lamassu is a powerful ancient Mesopotamian protective deity, appearing in art as a hybrid creature with a human head (symbolizing intelligence), the body of a bull or lion (strength), and eagle wings (freedom). Often featuring5 legs to appear stationary from the front and walking from the side, they served as monumental guardians at palace entrances.
